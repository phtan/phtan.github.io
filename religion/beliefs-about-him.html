What I believe

  • He created the universe and everything in it. He created the heavens and the earth.
    • He knows all, sees all and hears all.
  • He alone has authority on the Day of Judgment (other than those whom he gives authority to). In other words, He is sovereign over the Day of Judgment, or the Here-after.
  • He is the god mentioned in the Torah, the gospels and the Quran.
  • He is the god that the Nicene Creed refers to.
