About Tony in sixty words

Tony loves challenging himself to grow. Trained as a solo pianist, he enrolled in an harmonica ensemble, where he learnt about the interpersonal dynamics of making music in a group of colourful individuals.

In recent projects, he has striven to nurture the next generation of musicians who have the aptitude to perform in public venues, such as hospitals and libraries.
