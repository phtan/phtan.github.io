What am I doing now?

I'm giving thanks every day, or practising gratitude. It helps me feel better.

Sometimes I post about it on Twitter.

And I'm moderating and kick-starting discussions in a (online) chat-group about Japanese language and culture. (Under the organisation called Mundo Lingo).

This challenges me to improve my mastery of the Japanese language.

I'm deciding if I want to continue (ad-hoc) work at a hospital. The name of my job is "sitter", and I attend to patients, and help nurses out. Circumstances related to Covid - and people's reactions to Covid - give me pause.

Recently I've made effort to figure out what people want in such times. Trying to build - or cultivate - reciprocal relationships. "You need to give in order to receive," Derek Sivers wrote.
