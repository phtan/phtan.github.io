What am I doing now?

I've worked as a part-time barista for the past nine months or so. But I have resigned, to protect my mental health. I felt highly stressed at work, and I wondered: "is all this stress and misery necessary in my life?" I plan to take a break from conventional employment. I've contributed to society, through my barista-job, and now I need to rest.
