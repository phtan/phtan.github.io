Away to (hopefully) safety: A poem, written after reading Loung Ung's "First they killed my father"

Wordlessly,
I smacked the coffee-stain
on its rump-
And checked-
Was it gone?
Maybe, the sea - where I had sent it to - was not its home,
but then,
neither was I;
Or, I could no longer be
