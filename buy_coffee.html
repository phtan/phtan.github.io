Coffee

Like many people around the world, I drink coffee regularly.

So you want to buy me a coffee? Ordinarily, I would give you my Ko-fi details. But these times - especially since the pandemic began in the year 2020 - have been difficult for so many other people.

So I would like to invite you to give to other people instead:

  • Pro Bono SG provides legal advice to underprivileged communities in Singapore, members of which typically cannot afford a lawyer's services. examples of such comminities: migrant workers, and non-profit organisations.
  • Veterans International Cambodia makes and distributes wheelchairs to people who suffer from amputations, or loss of limbs, after a landmine has exploded near them. (The landmines were laid in the ground during military conflicts, decades ago. These landmines are nicknamed "steel soldiers" - they still remain lethal, without requiring sleep or food to sustain them).
