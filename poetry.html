Selected poems

No, my trip to the
sea-shore
wasn't a dream
See-
The sand on my feet-

Away to (hopefully) safety: A poem, written after reading Loung Ung's "First they killed my father", set in Pol Pot's Cambodia, rife with the "not-nice" Khmer Rouge, "destroyers of things"
Wordlessly,
I smacked the coffee-stain
on its rump-
And checked-
Was it gone?
Maybe, the sea - where I had sent it to - was not its home,
but then,
neither was I;
Or, I could no longer be
